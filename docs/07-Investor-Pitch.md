## Executive Summary

### The Opportunity

Manufacturing companies, particularly CNC factories, struggle with high operational overhead. A typical factory employs **6-15 office staff** to manage accounting, purchasing, sales support, and customer communication. This costs $300K-$750K annually in salaries alone, plus training, turnover, and inefficiencies.

### Our Solution

**Unified AI Business Assistant** is a comprehensive, cloud-based platform that consolidates all office operations into a single intelligent system. Our advanced AI automates **80-90% of routine tasks**, enabling **one person** to manage what previously required an entire team.

### The Market

- **Target Market:** 50,000+ CNC factories in North America alone
- **Market Size:** $15+ billion annually in operational costs
- **Growth:** Manufacturing sector digitization accelerating

### Why Now

1. **AI Maturity:** GPT-4, Claude, and other LLMs enable sophisticated automation
2. **Labor Costs:** Rising wages make automation ROI compelling
3. **Cloud Infrastructure:** Reliable, scalable, cost-effective
4. **Market Readiness:** Manufacturing companies seeking efficiency solutions
